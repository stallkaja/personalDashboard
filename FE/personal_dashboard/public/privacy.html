Privacy Policy

Stallkamp Family Dashboard · Last updated: 9 July 2026

The Stallkamp Family Dashboard ("the App") is a private, self-hosted family organizer. This policy explains what information the App handles and how it is used. The App is intended for use by members of our household who have been given an account.

Who runs this app

The App and its server are operated privately by the account holder. It is not a commercial service. Questions can be sent to jamesstallkamp@gmail.com.

Information the app handles

  • Account details — the name and email address you use to sign in, and a securely hashed password.
  • Content you create — calendar events, chores, meals, shopping lists, notes, and photos you choose to upload.
  • Notification tokens — if you enable push notifications, an anonymous browser/device push subscription so the App can send you reminders and alerts.
  • Location you set — an optional weather location you configure, used only to show local weather.

How the information is used

Information is used solely to provide the dashboard's features to your family. It is not sold, rented, or shared with advertisers or third parties, and it is not used for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Where data is stored

Data is stored on the operator's own self-hosted server and database. Weather forecasts are fetched from public weather services; no personal account data is sent to them. Push notifications are delivered through the standard web-push services provided by your device's browser vendor (e.g. Google, Apple, Mozilla).

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept for as long as your account is active. You may request that your account and associated data be deleted by contacting the operator at the email above; it will be removed from the server.

Children

The App is a private family tool. Any use by children is under the direct supervision and consent of the family's account holder.

Security

Access requires a personal login. Traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS), and passwords are stored only in hashed form.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new "last updated" date.
